About

Sport, data, technology, and people.

I am an Assistant Professor of Sport Management in the Department of Kinesiology at San José State University. My research examines how artificial intelligence, data analytics, and emerging technologies shape sport organizations, leadership, innovation, communication, and consumer behavior.

My work combines perspectives from sport management, data science, organizational behavior, and human-centered AI. I am particularly interested in how organizations can use technology responsibly while supporting effective decision-making and meaningful human experiences.

I co-lead the SPLASH Lab , which conducts interdisciplinary research across sport performance, psychology, learning, analytics, strategy, innovation, and human-centered artificial intelligence.
