About

Scholar, educator, and sport technology researcher.

I am an Assistant Professor of Sport Management in the Department of Kinesiology at San José State University. My research focuses on sport analytics, the application of artificial intelligence and big data in sport management, and sport technology innovation.

I collaborate with industry partners on research and student engagement initiatives, with a strong emphasis on experiential learning that connects students with real-world challenges in the sport industry.

In addition to my scholarly publications, I have presented my research at more than 40 national and international academic conferences, including conferences held in the United States, Canada, England, Norway, Hungary, Germany, Sweden, Spain, Russia, and South Korea.

Prior to joining San José State University, I served as a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Intercollegiate and Professional Sport Management program at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.

Before that, I held several academic leadership roles at Indiana University Bloomington, including Undergraduate Program Coordinator, Master's Program Coordinator, and Interim Doctoral Program Coordinator in Sport Management.

I earned my Ph.D. and M.S. in Sport Management from Indiana University Bloomington. I also hold an M.S. in Data Science from Indiana University's Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering.

My teaching portfolio includes courses in sport analytics, artificial intelligence, sport innovation, sport promotion and public relations, sport governance, international sport management, esports marketing, sport communication, and managing mega sport events.

I am a recipient of the Indiana University Trustees Teaching Award and the Paul R. Lawrence Fellowship from the North American Case Research Association. I have also served as principal investigator on several competitive research grants, including funding from the North American Society for Sport Management.

I currently serve on the editorial board of the International Journal of Sport Communication.

I co-lead the SPLASH Lab , an interdisciplinary research group focused on sport performance, psychology, learning, analytics, strategy, innovation, and human-centered artificial intelligence.
