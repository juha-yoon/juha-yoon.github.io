Research Vision

Sport, people, data, and technology.

I am an Assistant Professor of Sport Management in the Department of Kinesiology at San José State University. My research examines how artificial intelligence, analytics, and emerging technologies shape sport organizations, leadership, strategy, communication, and consumer experiences.

My work integrates perspectives from sport management, data science, organizational behavior, and human-centered AI. I am particularly interested in how organizations can adopt technology responsibly while supporting effective decisions, meaningful work, and positive human experiences.

I co-lead the SPLASH Lab , an interdisciplinary research group focused on sport performance, psychology, learning, analytics, strategy, innovation, and human-centered artificial intelligence.
